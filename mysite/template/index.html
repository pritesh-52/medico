Meet Dr. Vivek Jawali

Dr. Vivek Jawali is a well-known Cardiologist, who has an experience of more than 40 years of. He specializes in laparoscopic surgery, cardiac ablation, cardiac catheterization, carotid angioplasty & stenting, coronary angiogram, heart valve replacement, device closure, and several other procedures.He has performed more than 18,000 cardiothoracic & vascular surgeries so far.

He also performed India’s first beating heart bypass surgery in 1992, and India’s first minimally invasive bypass surgery in 1994. Dr. Jawali was also the first to perform Awake cardiac surgery in India in 1999.
